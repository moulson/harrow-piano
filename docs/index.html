About Me

I am a professional piano teacher with a decade of experience. I have a huge passion for music, dancing and helping young talents to fulfil their potentials. I love teaching young children to appreciate classical music and prepare for their ABRSM exams. I would love to go on the journey with more children and make a difference to the world through the love of music!

Lessons will be focus on the basics of piano technique, posture, hand position, the ability to read music notation, listening and aural skills, musicality and expression. These will provide the foundation for a life long adventure in piano playing. Most importantly, music is about having fun and being able to open up to the adventure that music can communicate to us.

FAQs

How much do you charge for lessons?

Lessons are charged at £18 for 30 minutes, £23 for 45 minutes and £28 for 60 minutes

Can I work towards a graded exam?

Yes, I am happy to help my students work through the ABRSM graded exams. It is also not a problem if you just want lessons for fun!

How long should my lessons be?

For beginners and younger students, lessons of 30-45 minutes are most productive. For those wishing to take their piano playing further or when approching examinations I would recommend 60 minutes.

What piano do you teach with?

I have an upright Steinmayer piano at my home that I use for teaching.

Do I need to have regular lessons?

My lessons can be flexible and I will try as best as I can to fit around a busy schedule. Regular lessons are the best way to see consistent improvement in piano playing however, I know that life can often get in the way. If you ever need to cancel or postpone a lesson that is fine.

When can I arrange lessons for?

I teach Saturdays and Sundays, please get in touch to see what times I have available

What experience do you have?

I have been teaching piano for more than 10 years both in Hong Kong and the UK. I have achieved my ABRSM Grade 8 Piano.
